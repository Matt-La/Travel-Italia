Things To Do...

  • Mountain Biking

    Don't miss out on an adventure of a lifetime.

    We have some of the best instrutors to navigator you round the hill sand mountains of Tuscany.

    For all the spider man enthusiasts, we have plenty of rocky surfaces to keep you entertained for weeks.

    If you want to just chill out, surrounded by the beauty of the mountains and the calm of the still lake water then look no further.

    You have the choice to either paddle in and around the drop off location or we can paddle back to the hotel complex.
